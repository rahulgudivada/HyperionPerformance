    Hyperion Performance is a not for profit consulting group specializing in financial, technology, and media consulting built by Chicago college students for college students. We give students the opportunity to work and consult for businesses in the Chicagoland area and allow for them to gain real world experience. Businesses are able to gain from the implementation of modern business principles at no cost at all.
